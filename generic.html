History of Insane-Zone

Insane-Zone is a Romanian Minecraft Bedrock Edition Server founded by Eduard on May 20, 2020.

The Dev learns how to code step by step, until the Dev knows basic codes such as If Else Condition etc. Insane-Zone is also known as INS for alias name.

How to Play?

Insane-Zone is a Hybrid Theme Server. Meaning, Insane-Zone is All in One Server. Like Other Servers, Insane-Zone have Acidlands, Factions, SkyPvP in only One Server!

Townie Hub [Lobby]

Townie is The Main Spawn for Insane-Zone, You can choose either you enter to SkyPvPor Factions or Play Games like Duels, Etc.

Factions [Town]

Factions is a Survival Theme but a Raidable Gamemode
All You Need to do is to Make A Base and Keep your Items from your designated Chest.
Beware at Raiders! Keep Safe.

AcidIslands[Sky]

AcidIslands is a Survival Theme but on a island
All you need to do is to survive the island and make it bigger

SkyPvP [Skys]

In SkyPvP you have to get materials from islands to make an armor and fight for more with other players
Dont worry, you cant cheat here or get items to faction because the SkyPvP is protected by exploiters

Arcade Games [Arc] (Soon)

Arcade Games is a Fun Theme, You can play SkyWars or BedWars here made by Local Devs
